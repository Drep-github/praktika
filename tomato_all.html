Agridera Fresh Market Bananas

×××××

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of banana varieties. We invest intensively in research and development to provide innovative solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries worldwide. Our focus is on developing high-yielding, disease-resistant, and climate-adapted banana cultivars for different markets. Currently, our breeding efforts are concentrated on developing resistance to major banana diseases such as Panama Disease (Fusarium wilt TR4), Black Sigatoka, and Banana Bunchy Top Virus (BBTV). Additionally, we are working on improving traits such as shelf life, fruit size, taste, and adaptability to various environmental conditions. Our breeding programs target multiple banana types, including dessert bananas (Cavendish alternatives), plantains, and cooking bananas, to meet the diverse needs of global markets.
Agridera’s banana breeding programs focus on three main segments: Greenhouse Bananas, Open-field Commercial Bananas, and Smallholder Farming Bananas.

Greenhouse Banana Breeding Program

This program is aimed at high-tech greenhouse production, particularly for premium markets in Europe and the Middle East. The focus is on compact, high-yielding plants with resistance to Panama Disease (TR4), Black Sigatoka, and BBTV. Our hybrids are developed to have improved post-harvest durability, enhanced sweetness, and uniform fruit quality.

Open-field Commercial Bananas

Designed for large-scale plantations in Latin America, Africa, and Southeast Asia, this breeding program focuses on high-yielding varieties with resistance to Fusarium TR4, Black Sigatoka, and Moko disease. We also develop longer shelf-life varieties suitable for international export markets.

Smallholder Farming Bananas

This program is tailored for small and medium-scale farmers in South Asia and Africa, where bananas are a staple food. The breeding priorities include disease resistance (BBTV, Fusarium TR4, Black Sigatoka), drought tolerance, and adaptability to low-input farming. We develop both dessert and cooking bananas that maintain excellent taste and texture while being highly productive under local farming conditions.
Agridera operates through international and local distributors worldwide, ensuring that growers have access to high-quality, improved banana varieties adapted to their specific regions.
